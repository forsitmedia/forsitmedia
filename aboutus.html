About Us

ForSit comes from the Latin fors sit — “let it be fate.”
Together we create a true connection and authentic content which helps real people choose you.

Our Story

We started ForSit about a year and a half ago while finishing university. As creators, we saw great businesses (like yours) getting ignored online— not for lack of quality, but for lack of a clear, authentic presence. We began helping them show up the right way.

Since then, we’ve produced in Italy, Argentina, Portugal, and Switzerland, upgrading our gear and sharpening our process. Sometimes we directed from one country while a trusted shooter filmed in another—then we edited and delivered seamlessly. Now we’re all-in. If you join us now, you’re part of building something bigger.

Who We Are

We’re Filippo de Cristofaro (Management & Entrepreneurship — Nova SBE & LUISS) and Luna Darch (Global Communication). We’ve run on personal TikTok/IG accounts with millions of views and partnered with brands—then chose to pour that energy into our clients.

We speak English, Portuguese, Spanish, and Italian, and we’ve lived across these countries. Filippo brings bold ideas and hands-on craft; Luna brings sharp messaging and organized delivery. Together: creative + precise.

Our Mission

To make life easier for business owners by creating authentic content that connects people to what you do best. We help brands show up clearly, beautifully, and truthfully online— without confusion, tricks, or wasted effort.

Our Vision

To become the leading media company in Europe, expanding fast and setting new standards for creativity and trust. Our goal is to make every client we work with the most successful and followed in their industry, turning visibility into lasting growth.
